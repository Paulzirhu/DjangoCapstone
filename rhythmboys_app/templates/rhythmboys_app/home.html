Welcome to Rhythm Boys

Influenced by R&B, rap, and buoyant pop music, the American boy band Mindless Behavior formed in Los Angeles in 2008. The initial lineup featured Jacob Anthony Perez (also know as "Princeton"), Chresanto Romelo Lorenzo August ("Roc Royal"), Rayan De'Quan Lopez ("Ray Ray"), and Craig Thomas Crippen Jr..
